Bottle Art

Info

It's transforming old wine bottles into stunning vases, crafting whimsical lanterns from recycled glass bottles, or creating intricate sculptures from found objects, bottle art celebrates the beauty of upcycling and the art of turning ordinary items into extraordinary works of art.

Oil-Painting

info

Oil painting is a versatile and timeless medium that has been used by artists for centuries to create stunning works of art.With its rich history and timeless appeal, oil painting continues to inspire artists around the world to create beautiful and captivating works of art.

Paper Boat

Info

If you want to make a paper boat, fold a piece of paper in half from top to bottom. Then, fold it again from side to side and unfold so you have a crease in the center. Fold the top right corner down towards the center. Next, flip the paper over and fold the other corner down on the other side.

My Morning Routine


Wake up naturally: Allow yourself to wake up without an alarm clock, enjoying the luxury of sleeping in a bit later if you'd like.

or yoga: Start your day with some gentle stretching or yoga to wake up your body and improve flexibility.

Healthy breakfast: Enjoy a leisurely breakfast with your family, perhaps trying out some new recipes or making a special meal together.

Outdoor time: Spend some time outdoors, whether it's playing in the yard, going for a walk, or riding your bike. Fresh air and sunshine can do wonders for your mood and energy levels.

Read or engage in a hobby: Set aside some time for reading, drawing, playing a musical instrument, or working on a hobby you enjoy.

Screen time: If you plan to spend time watching TV, playing video games, or using electronic devices set limits to ensure you have a balanced day.

Family time: Spend quality time with your family, whether it's playing board games, cooking together, or having a movie night.

Relaxation: Don't forget to take some time to relax and unwind. You might want to listen to music, take a nap, or simply sit quietly and enjoy some downtime.

My Daily Routine


Daytime:

Engage in various activities such as going to the park, playing sports, riding bikes, swimming, or going to museums or other attractions. Spend time on creative activities like drawing, painting, crafting, or building with LEGO. Read books for pleasure or work on personal projects or interests. Help out with household chores or tasks if needed. Take breaks throughout the day for snacks and relaxation.

Afternoon/Evening:

Have lunch with family or friends. Continue with planned activities or outings. Play board games or video games with siblings or friends. Watch movies or TV shows. Engage in family activities like cooking together, going for walks, or visiting relatives. Reflect on the day's activities and maybe journal about memorable experiences. Prepare for bed with a bedtime routine similar to the school routine, ensuring enough sleep for the next day's adventures.
